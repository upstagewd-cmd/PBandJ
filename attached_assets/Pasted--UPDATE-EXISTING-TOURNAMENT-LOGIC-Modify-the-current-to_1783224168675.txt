# UPDATE EXISTING TOURNAMENT LOGIC

Modify the current tournament implementation. This is a change request to the existing app.

## REMOVE DOUBLE ELIMINATION

Completely remove all double elimination functionality.

Delete:
- Losers bracket
- Consolation bracket
- Second-chance bracket
- Any logic that advances losing teams into another tournament bracket

There should only be one bracket in the application.

---

# SINGLE ELIMINATION ONLY

The tournament should now be:

- Single elimination
- Winners advance
- Losers are eliminated from the championship bracket immediately

Once a team loses, they should never appear in another tournament bracket.

---

# CREATE AN OPEN PLAY SECTION

Instead of a losers bracket, create a new section directly below the tournament bracket called:

## 🏓 Open Play

This is not a tournament.

This is not a bracket.

This is simply a live area where players can continue playing casual games after they have been eliminated.

---

# WHO APPEARS IN OPEN PLAY

As soon as a team loses a tournament match:

1. Remove them from the tournament bracket.
2. Automatically add them to the Open Play section.

Open Play should display available teams with:
- Player names
- Profile pictures (if available)

---

# OPEN PLAY MATCHES

Users should be able to manually record games played in Open Play.

When selecting Record Match, allow users to choose:

- Team 1
- Team 2
- Winning Team
- Optional Score (example: 11–8)

When the match is saved:

- Update each player's win/loss record
- Update player ratings
- Update player statistics
- Update badges if applicable
- Save the match to each player's history

---

# IMPORTANT

Open Play should NOT:

- Generate brackets
- Eliminate teams
- Determine a champion
- Automatically schedule games
- Automatically pair teams
- Assign courts

Players decide who they want to play in real life.

The app simply records the results.

---

# OPEN PLAY SHOULD ALWAYS REMAIN AVAILABLE

The Open Play section should remain visible throughout the tournament.

As more teams are eliminated, they are added to the Open Play list automatically.

By the time the championship match is being played, every eliminated team should already be available in Open Play and able to continue playing casual games.

---

# USER EXPERIENCE GOAL

The page should have two clear sections:

## 🏆 Championship Tournament

Single elimination bracket only.

This section determines the tournament winner.

---

## 🏓 Open Play

Casual games for everyone eliminated from the tournament.

Users manually log games and scores.

These games count toward:
- Player win/loss records
- Hidden player ratings
- Rankings
- Badges
- Match history

Open Play never affects the tournament bracket or championship.

---

# SUCCESS CRITERIA

The experience should feel like a real pickleball meetup:

- Everyone starts in the tournament.
- Teams that lose immediately move into Open Play.
- Nobody sits around waiting.
- Everyone continues playing.
- The tournament continues until one champion remains.
- Open Play continues independently until people decide to stop playing.
